  • A triplet is composed of 3 elements: Entity 1, Action, Entity 2.
  • The entities are legal references to laws, represented in the form of a court decision, a directive, a regulation, etc.
  • Actions are represented as verbs: To ammend, to repeal , to implement, etc.
